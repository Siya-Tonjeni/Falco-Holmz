Falco Holmz

Falco Holmz, born Zazi Ntozini, is a dynamic and innovative South African music artist hailing from Mthatha in the Eastern Cape. His musical journey began in a youth choir, but it was his move to Johannesburg and subsequent experiences that truly shaped his artistic path. Initially starting as a rapper,

Falco quickly discovered a unique sound that blends Alternative/Indie with Hip-Hop and Afro styles, drawing inspiration from a diverse array of artists such as Ms. Lauryn Hill, Andre 3000, The Black Eyed Peas, The Soil, Freshly Ground, and Matthew Mole.

First Release

His debut EP, "4 Peace Sake, " released in November 2021, marked his official entry into the music scene, featuring standout tracks like "Echo, " "Autopilot, " "Worth It, " and "Silos. " These songs set the stage for his evolving artistry, resonating with fans and establishing him as a crowd favorite. His music is celebrated for its authenticity, emotional depth, and the seamless fusion of genres.
