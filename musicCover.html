4 Peace Sake: II - EP

"4 Peace Sake: II" solidifies Falco Holmz's position as a compelling storyteller and a versatile musical force. This EP offers listeners an intimate glimpse into his personal growth and artistic evolution, delivering relatable narratives with a captivating and genre-defying sound. Find My Way - Single

25 OCTOBER, 2024

"Find My Way" is a raw and honest anthem of resilience, chronicling Falco's journey from the Eastern Cape to Johannesburg, where he overcame personal struggles to pursue his dreams and find his path.

UNGOWAMI - Single

25 SEPTEMBER , 2023

"Ungowami," Falco Holmz's Afro-pop single in his native language isiXhosa, marks a significant milestone in his artistic journey. This unique offering diverges from his familiar alternative pop sound, showcasing his versatility and cultural roots. "Ungowami" tells a heartfelt love story, depicting a man's triumphant search for true love after years of longing.

Masterpiece - Single

26 MAY, 2023

"Masterpiece" is a bold departure from Falco Holmz's usual sound, diving deep into the realm of Hip-Hop. This collaboration with Caleb Kalz showcases a more confident and assertive side of the artist. The song is a celebration of talent and ambition, with lyrics that exude bravado and self-belief. Falco's journey from aspiring rapper to versatile artist has culminated in this powerful track. "Masterpiece" is a testament to his growth and evolution as a musician. The song's deeper meaning lies in Falco's recognition of his unique gifts and his determination to use them to make a lasting impact.

Adorn - Single

15 JULY, 2022

A captivating blend of pop, dance, and hip-hop, "Adorn" delves into the glamorous side of love. Inspired by the concept of making something more beautiful, Falco Holmz and Chrxs James create a smooth, groovy track that celebrates intimacy and desire. With its infectious hook and melodic lyrics, "Adorn" explores the romantic side of love, where a man expresses his appreciation for a special woman. Whether it's through love or material possessions, the song highlights the desire to adorn and cherish one's partner.

Echo - Single

05 NOVEMBER, 2021

This was not originally mine and was the only song recorded before the COVID-19 pandemic. At the time of recording I was still finding my style as a new comer. This is the first time Falco Holmz was unleashed and I’ve never looked back. I wrote the song from a perspective of a broken man looking to put the pieces back together but it’s too late.It took the longest time to finish the song due to unforeseen circumstances. At the end, the song later on became my debut single.
