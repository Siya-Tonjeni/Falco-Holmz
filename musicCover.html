UNGOWAMI - Single

25 SEPTEMBER , 2023

"Ungowami," Falco Holmz's Afro-pop single in his native language isiXhosa, marks a significant milestone in his artistic journey. This unique offering diverges from his familiar alternative pop sound, showcasing his versatility and cultural roots. "Ungowami" tells a heartfelt love story, depicting a man's triumphant search for true love after years of longing.

Echo - Single

05 NOVEMBER, 2021

This was not originally mine and was the only song recorded before the COVID-19 pandemic. At the time of recording I was still finding my style as a new comer. This is the first time Falco Holmz was unleashed and I’ve never looked back. I wrote the song from a perspective of a broken man looking to put the pieces back together but it’s too late.It took the longest time to finish the song due to unforeseen circumstances. At the end, the song later on became my debut single.
